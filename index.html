The Story

Frank Does What He Said He Would.

Your home is your biggest investment. It's not just bricks and mortar — it's personal. But here in Australia, tradies have no shortage of work. And when demand is high, standards can slide. Homeowners end up paying good money for average outcomes, with no idea what questions to ask or what their rights even are.

Frank is named after my grandfather — Frank Hall, a builder who worked from the 1950s right through to the '80s. He was well respected. Not because he was flashy or clever, but because he was straight-talking and utterly reliable. He showed up. He did what he said he'd do. He believed, to his core, that you're only as good as your word and your actions.

That's the Frank we built. Frank is online, on your side, and ready to help you navigate the whole process — so you know the rules, spot the red flags early, ask the right questions at the right time, and walk away with exactly what you paid for.
